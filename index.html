Fall Programs

Cadet Flyers

Cadet Flyers is a skills based training program where female athletes in grades 4 and 5 will develop in a fun yet challenging learning environment. The focus will be on forearm passing, setting, serving and hitting, using Gold Medal Squared coaching methods. It is expected that athletes in this program will not have had any exposure to volleyball instruction, outside of school PE class. Instruction will be tailored toward providing the maximum possible ball touches during the session.

Junior Flyers

Junior Flyers is similar to Cadet Flyers, except that it is geared towards grade 6 and 7 athletes that are participating in extra-curricular school volleyball teams and may be considering trying out for a 12U or 13U club team in November. The pace of instruction will be more brisk and will include more advanced skills, including defensive digging, blocking and proper footwork.

Flight School

Flight School is geared towards athletes with previous club or extensive school volleyball experience and will be focused on consolidating proper skills, as well as correcting bio-mechanical deficiencies that can limit performance and create injuries. Coaching feedback will be intensive, and will be supplemented with slow-motion video review using the Hudl Technique app. Advanced topics such as defensive diving and digging, serve receive, cue reading and swing blocking will be covered.
