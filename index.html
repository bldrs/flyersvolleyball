About Us

Flyers Volleyball Club serves the eastern Fraser Valley communities of Abbotsford, Chilliwack, Mission and Aldergrove. Our mission is to provide a great competitive volleyball experience for girls in our area, and to develop a skilled and knowledgeable team of coaches that is capable of providing that experience.

2019 Tryouts

Take Flight

We appreciate your interest in being part of the Flyers family for the 2019 season! The Flyers Volleyball club believes in creating an atmosphere for athletes to excel in an environment that is both intense and fun. We aim to provide opportunities for athletes to develop their technical abilities, never losing sight of the importance of also building strong interpersonal relationships, leadership skills and engaging with our community. We look forward to building and working with our 2019 teams!

A few Important details regarding tryouts!

  • Please arrive 20 minutes early to allow adequate time to complete the necessary paperwork
  • Tryout fees are non-refundable
  • Tryout fee covers both tryouts. It is recommended that athletes attend both if possible. If athletes can only attend one, please contact us in advance of the tryouts.
  • Athletes who have previously played for a different club DO NOT need to provide an athlete release form
  • Athletes offered a position on a team need to accept the offer by 6pm on the date noted below. If an athlete does not accept by the prescribed time / date, the offer no longer stands:
    • 13U: November 26, 2018
    • 14U: November 26, 2018
    • 15U: December 3, 2018
    • 16/17U: December 10, 2018

Questions: If you have any questions at all please email: admin@flyersvolleyball.com

UFV Cascades

UFV Cascades Women’s Volleyball Team

Flyers Volleyball is continuing to develop an affinity program with the University of the Fraser Valley Cascades. The program includes components such as athlete mentorship, coaching development and fundraising. It is our belief that the growing synergy between Flyers Volleyball and the UFV Cascades will help both organizations become stronger and will ultimately help us grow the game of volleyball in the eastern Fraser Valley.
